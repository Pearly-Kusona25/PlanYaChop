About PlanYaChop AI

Built for real kitchens, busy schedules, and practical food planning.

PlanYaChop turns ingredients, time, and budget constraints into meals you can actually cook. The platform is designed to reduce planning friction from discovery to shopping and prep.

What we solve

Most meal apps assume unlimited time and ingredients. PlanYaChop focuses on constraints first, then builds useful recipe and meal-plan outputs from what is available right now.

How we approach AI

AI suggestions are structured around prep time, cook time, serving targets, and ingredient reuse. The goal is clear execution, not vague ideas.

Who it is for

Home cooks, students, families, and small food operators who need reliable weekly planning, recipe access, and shopping lists in one workflow.
